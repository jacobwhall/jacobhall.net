Help Preserve HBW

Let's stitch together everyone's photos of our school

A project of The H-B Archive

The Idea

Everyone takes a few photos of a senior wall, mural, or other H-B landmark

Senior wall photos are stitched together, and other photos are made available online

We can all enjoy our shared history!

What Photos are Needed?

  • Senior Walls!
  • Photos of classrooms, landmarks
  • Photos of students and events

Tips for Taking Rad Photos

Try to
git clone /path/to/repository
If you can, capture the wall in good light
If you're taking a photo of someone, make sure you have permission!

What will you do with my photos?

Your photos will be stitched into a big quilt of photos that people submit, to
create one massive photo of each senior wall and mural. Everywhere that the
stitched photo is made available, your name will be attributed as a creator.
