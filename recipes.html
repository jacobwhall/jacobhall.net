pancakes

dirt-simple pancakes, one batch fills me up.

ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • almost 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • tiny lil splash of vanilla extract (optional)
  • lil bit of honey

steps

  1. mix all the ingredients in a mixing bowl,
    adding milk if its a little too thick
  2. put oil in pan and let it heat up a bit on medium-low
  3. cook pancakes
